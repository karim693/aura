Our Story

AURA was founded in 2026 with a simple mission: to create unforgettable fragrances that capture moments and emotions. Our perfumers travel the globe to source the most exquisite ingredients, from Bulgarian roses to Indian sandalwood.

Each fragrance in our collection is crafted with precision and passion, using both traditional techniques and modern innovation. We believe that a perfume should not just smell good, but also tell a story and evoke memories.

Today, AURA is recognized as one of the leading perfume houses worldwide, with our creations adorning the dressing tables of fragrance connoisseurs in over 50 countries.
